ESD Ionizers

ESD ionizers, also known as static neutralizers or static eliminators, are devices used to control and neutralize static electricity in environments where electrostatic discharge (ESD) can pose a risk to sensitive electronic components or processes. Ionizers work by emitting ions, which are charged particles, to neutralize static charges on surfaces and in the air. This helps to prevent the buildup of static electricity and reduces the likelihood of ESD events. ESD ionizers are commonly used in industries such as electronics manufacturing, semiconductor fabrication, aerospace, and healthcare, where static control is critical to prevent damage to sensitive equipment and products. By neutralizing static charges, ionizers help to minimize the risk of electrostatic discharge and ensure the reliability and quality of electronic components and assemblies.
