ESD Brushes & ESD File Holders

ESD (Electrostatic Discharge) brushes are specialized brushes designed to safely remove static electricity and particulate contamination from surfaces, components, or workpieces in environments where electrostatic discharge could cause damage to sensitive electronic devices or assemblies. These brushes are commonly used in industries such as electronics manufacturing, cleanroom operations, and telecommunications, where static control and cleanliness are essential. ESD brushes are valuable tools for maintaining a static-safe and clean environment in electronic manufacturing facilities, cleanrooms, and other settings where electrostatic discharge and particulate contamination could compromise product quality and reliability. By using ESD brushes, manufacturers can minimize the risk of ESD-related damage and ensure the integrity of their electronic products and assemblies. ESD (Electrostatic Discharge) file holders are specialized holders or organizers designed to store and organize documents, papers, or other materials while providing protection against electrostatic discharge. These holders are commonly used in environments where static control is essential, such as electronics manufacturing facilities, cleanrooms, and laboratories.
