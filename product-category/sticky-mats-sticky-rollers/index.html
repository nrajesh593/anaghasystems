Sticky Mats & Sticky Rollers

Sticky mats, also known as adhesive mats or tacky mats, are specialized mats used to remove dirt, dust, and particulate contamination from footwear and wheels before entering cleanroom or controlled environments. These mats are commonly used in industries such as electronics manufacturing, semiconductor fabrication, pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, healthcare, and laboratories, where maintaining cleanliness and minimizing contamination is essential. Overall, sticky mats are valuable tools for minimizing contamination and maintaining cleanliness in controlled environments. By trapping and removing dirt and particulate matter from footwear and wheels, sticky mats help to preserve the integrity of products, processes, and equipment in industries where cleanliness is paramount. Sticky rollers, also known as adhesive rollers or tacky rollers, are specialized tools used for removing dust, lint, hair, and other particulate contaminants from surfaces such as clothing, furniture, carpets, and floors. These rollers are particularly useful in environments where cleanliness is essential, such as cleanrooms, laboratories, manufacturing facilities, and households. sticky rollers are practical and efficient tools for removing dust and debris from surfaces in a variety of settings. Whether used in cleanrooms, laboratories, manufacturing facilities, or homes, sticky rollers help maintain cleanliness and hygiene by quickly and easily capturing contaminants without leaving behind residues or scratches.
