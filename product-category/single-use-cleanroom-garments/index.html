Single Use Cleanroom Garments

Anagha Systems is a leader in Indian market for the manufacture and supply of Cleanroom Workwear. In any Cleanroom Environment, still human body is considered as greatest source of contamination. Wearing Cleanroom Garments is mandatory for an individual in Cleanrooms to prevent natural spread of contamination from wearers body, controlling particle count and ultimately improve the product quality. Cleanroom Garments used should adhere to the Cleanroom classification and its quality standards. Cleanrooms are classified by how clean the air is, particles permitted per volume of air. Often Cleanroom standards are referred to by the relevant class derived from the ISO 14644-1 standards. As the Reusable Cleanroom Garments are made of precisely made Anti-Static Fabric (98% Polyester & 2% Conductive Carbon Grid), ESD (Electro Static Discharge) Standards ANSI S20:20 can be considered for analysis. Conductive Carbon Grid will aid to dissipate the static charges during routine operations inside Cleanroom. With a significant experience of more than a decade, we understand the cleanroom requirements of Pharmaceutical, Biotechnology, Chemical and Electronics Manufacturing Industries in India.

Our in-depth knowledge of today’s Industry Standards and Cleanroom Garment requirements, allows us to quickly respond to customer’s request and fulfil their tailored fit requirements with shorter lead times. Anagha Autoclavable Cleanroom Garments are manufactured with quality proven best in class imported fabrics and elements (Ex. Zipper, Thread, Stud, etc.,) with optimised design for wearer comfort and assured performance throughout its life. It is very important that Cleanroom Garments be sized correctly. Improperly sized garments can raise the level of contaminants and cause safety concerns. Our in-house design expert team, own manufacturing facility and highly qualified quality control team assure the quality of product by exceeding customer’s expectations. We provide a consistent and professional service to our customers.

Anagha Cleanroom Workwear quality is being certified every day by an increasing number of satisfied wearers. Our product quality will help you stay ahead of your competition.
