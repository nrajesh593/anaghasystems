ESD Testers

ESD testers, also known as electrostatic discharge testers or ESD testing equipment, are devices used to evaluate and measure the electrostatic discharge (ESD) characteristics of electronic components, devices, and materials. These testers are crucial tools in ensuring that products comply with industry standards for ESD protection and safety. Our ESD testers includes Wrist Strap Testers, Surface Resistivity Meters, Surface Resistance Test Kit, Megohm Meters, Dual Footwear / Wrist Strap Testers, etc., ESD testers are essential for maintaining the reliability and integrity of electronic products by ensuring they are adequately protected from electrostatic discharge. Manufacturers rely on these testers to comply with industry standards and regulations for ESD control and prevention.
