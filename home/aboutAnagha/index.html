About Anagha

Who Are We

Anagha Systems is one of fastest growing supplier for ESD Anti Static & Conductive Products, Cleanroom Workwear, Cleanroom Wipes, Cleanroom Gloves and Sterilization Reels & Pouches and best of solutions for Electronics Manufacturing, Pharmaceuticals, Chemical, Food, Oil industries in INDIA.

We believe in growing our business by providing value added services & products to our customers. We listen to our customers and work hard to deliver beyond their expectations. At the same time we are continually looking to the future and working to source innovative products globally & supply locally at ex-stocks prices in INDIA.

With wide range of products, we are proud to cater Electronics Manufacturing,Pharmaceuticals, Chemical, Food, Oil industries, Automobile, Telecom, Semiconductor, Defense, Aerospace, Power and Industrial sectors.
